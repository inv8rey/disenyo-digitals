The AYA Builder's Circle is an intimate, facilitated gathering designed to help Baguio entrepreneurs, creators, and community builders connect meaningfully, share challenges, and find collaborators. Expect structured rounds, real conversation, and zero corporate awkwardness — just people building things, being real about it.
